Te Whare Tapa Wha

Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Go to the gym early in the morning at least 6 times a week
Taha Whanau (Family Wellbeing) Meeting with my brother at least once a week to make sure he's doing well. Also checking in with my parents with a phone call.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional Wellbeing) Meditate at least once a day, start on a small one then make my way up to longer ones. Prefrebbly in the morning before work and after gym
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Practice mindfulness and write down my thoughts, spend more time outside and in nature to try and reset instead of sitting inside all the time
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) One of my excercies during the week must be outside such as a run or yoga on the lawn
