Emotional Intellegince

  • Emotional Intellegince to me is knowing your own emotional capability and being able to see others. Being able to help a friend or someone through a tough time and empathise with them to me is a big part of EQ.

  • Working through the stress and tough times, strengthing your mind through excercises so that you can get through hard tasks that you thought you couldn't do.

  • Being able to work towards resolutions when there is split between the group and understanding both sides then trying to find a way for both parties to work together.

  • I feel like I have started trying to strengthen my mental habits by practicing meditation in the morning and clearing my mind. My mind is always so busy and having a few minutes to calm it down really helps.
